# Settings and Preferences

Manage your account, preferences, and system settings.

## Accessing Settings

1. Click **"Settings"** in the left sidebar
2. Or click your name in the top right â†’ **Settings**
3. Settings view opens with tabs

## Account Tab

### Profile Information
- **Name**: Your display name
- **Email**: Your email address (used for login)
- **Role**: Your role (admin, coach, viewer)

### Password Management
- **Change Password**: Update your password
- Requires current password
- New password must meet requirements

### Account Actions
- **Logout**: Sign out of your account
- Session ends immediately

## Preferences Tab

### Game Data Metrics Reset
- **Reset Metrics to Defaults**: Restore default metric selections
- Clears your custom metric preferences
- Returns to system defaults
- Confirmation required

### Chart Preferences
- Chart customizations are saved automatically
- No manual save needed
- Preferences persist across sessions

## Custom Charts Tab (Admin/Coaches)

### Creating Custom Charts
1. Click **"Create Custom Chart"**
2. Select metrics to include
3. Choose chart type
4. Configure options
5. Save your chart

### Managing Custom Charts
- **Edit**: Modify existing custom charts
- **Delete**: Remove custom charts
- **Use**: Select in chart dropdowns

## Data at a Glance Tab

### Quick Overview
- Summary statistics
- Key metrics at a glance
- Team performance overview

## Users Tab (Admin Only)

### User Management
- **View Users**: See all system users
- **Create User**: Add new users
- **Edit User**: Modify user details
- **Delete User**: Remove users (with safeguards)

### User Roles
- **Admin**: Full system access
- **Coach**: Data entry and analysis
- **Viewer**: Read-only access

## Teams Tab (Admin Only)

### Team Management
- **View Teams**: See all teams
- **Create Team**: Add new teams
- **Edit Team**: Modify team details
- **Delete Team**: Remove teams (with safeguards)

### Team Information
- Team name and slug
- Season association
- Parent/child relationships
- Team aliases

## Tips

1. **Keep Profile Updated**: Ensure your information is current
2. **Use Strong Passwords**: Protect your account
3. **Save Preferences**: Customize to your needs
4. **Regular Review**: Check settings periodically
